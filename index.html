What do we do?

We are currently working on designing and building our mini F1 car. It will be make out of polystyrene foam in the most part, with some 3D printed elements added on. We will race it against other teams from other schools around the country, and it will be powered by a canister of compressed C02 in the back.

Initial Livery Idea

This is the car we used to win our internal competition and our basis for the livery we will use going forward. We are very happy with how it looks, and will use a similar livery for most, if not all, of our future cars.
