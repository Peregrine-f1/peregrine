Breaking the Limits

What are we?

We are Peregrine, an F1 in Schools team. We are currently competing in Entry Class in the 2024/25 season. .

What are we?

We are currently working on designing anf building our mini F1 car. It will be make out of polystyrene foam in the most part, with some 3D printed elements added on. We will race it against other teams from other schools around the country, and it will be powered by a canister of compressed C02 in the back. .

Initial Livery Idea

This is the car we used to win our internal competition and our base for the livery we will use going forward. We are very happy with how good it looks, and we proabably won't change it much as the competition continues.
